Still Hanoi Move
digital photos
2011
STATEMENT

Taken during my 12 week stay in the bustling capitol of Vietnam, this digital street photography depicts people using various means of transportation. Still Hanoi Move abstracts or visually stutters the motion and image, as with the shadow silhouettes of Spin III (Other Side) and Wild Ride, or with the panoramic shots Walk Out, Rushing Waiting, and (((()). By exaggerating movement or finding still moments in the midst of streaking cars, Still Hanoi Move both reigns in and travels with the motion of the city.
