Genesis & Dreamtime
oil on canvas and watercolors
2011—2012
STATEMENT

While my current work is interdisciplinary, my early work was almost exclusively painting. In Genesis & Dreamtime, I developed my own abstract symbols to create paintings based on the Judaeo-Christian creation narrative. The forms I developed, inspired by cellular biology and oceanic forms, as well as the emphasis on the growth process, shaped my later work.
