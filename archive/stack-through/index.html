2011
STATEMENT

This project was an early venture into creating work out of found objects from my local community, a method I am currently pursuing with YESTERDAY, TODAY, TOMORROW: glory. These catalog card cut outs also led me to the cut out elements of my undergraduate show, As I Am, Changing, as well as other explorations of layers of overlapping text, as with WHITE WHITE RED: living woman.
